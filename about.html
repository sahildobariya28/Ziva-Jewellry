Our Legacy

Founded in 1985, RKDDT Diamonds is a family-owned business that has been a pioneer in fine jewellery. With decades of expertise, we have redefined the jewellery industry by offering exceptional craftsmanship, exquisite designs, and unmatched value.

Beauty in Every Stone.

Blending tradition with modern flair, we bring you elegant gold and gemstone designs.Wear jewelry that empowers you—confidence begins with how you shine.

From Heart to Hand – Pure Brilliance

Created with passion and crafted with care—our pieces are gifts from the heart.Let your personality shine through unique stones that reflect who you are.every design speaks of personal journeys, milestones, and cherished memories.

Innovation & Craftsmanship

Today, RKDDT Diamonds is recognized for its superior jewellery and exceptional customer service. By embracing cutting-edge technology, we provide a personalized and immersive design experience. Our clients can bring their unique visions to life with computer-aided designs (CAD) and 3D renderings, ensuring each piece is truly one of a kind.
